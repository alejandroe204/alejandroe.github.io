Hi there,
I’m Alejandro Escalante!


a passionate graphic designer currently pursuing my bachelor’s degree in graphic design at UNITEC. My journey in design began at a young age with a fascination for art, since then, I’ve dedicated myself to learning and creating work that is visually appealing and that make a connection with people.

My design philosophy centers around the belief that great design is a balance of aesthetics and functionality. I love exploring new ways to bring ideas to life, whether it's through new techniques or new methods. Collaboration is key for me, and I find inspiration in working closely with others to create something meaningful.

When I’m not designing, you can find me sketching, listening to music, or enjoying a good movie. I’m always looking forward to learning and take on new challenges, and I'm excited to continue growing as a designer.

Let's connect and create something amazing together!
